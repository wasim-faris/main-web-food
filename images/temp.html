Welcome to bake story

Nestled in the heart of Mittai theruvu (S.M. Street), Sankaran Bakery has been spreading sweetness for generations since its humble beginnings in 1933. Renowned for our traditional recipes and authentic Kozhikodan sweets, we take pride in being a part of the rich history and culture of Kerala's iconic sweet street.

A Legacy of Tradition and Taste

Founded by Sankaran Vaidyar, Sankaran Bakery has preserved the original recipes and techniques that make our sweets a cherished part of Kozhikode's culinary heritage. Now managed by Mr. Ramesh, the third generation of the family, the bakery continues to be a landmark for locals and visitors alike.
